About Me

Professional Photographer from Chikmagalur


Hello, I'm Akshay. For over 15 years, I've had the privilege of capturing life's most precious moments for couples and families across Chikmagalur and beyond. To me, photography is more than just taking pictures—it's about preserving emotions, telling stories, and creating memories that last a lifetime. From the quiet glances between loved ones to the joyful laughter shared during celebrations, I strive to capture every genuine moment with creativity and passion. Whether it's an intimate engagement, a grand wedding, a family gathering, or a portrait session, my goal is to transform your special moments into timeless photographs that you will cherish forever. Every story is unique, and I am dedicated to capturing yours with authenticity, artistry, and attention to detail.


My mission is to provide a seamless, comfortable experience that allows your genuine emotions to shine, transforming fleeting moments into timeless visual stories through creative photography.
